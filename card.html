Bengal

This cat has a special form of camouflage fur, a remembrance of its wild ancestors...

  • Scientific Name: Prionailurus bengalensis
  • Average Lifespan: 14-16 years
  • Habitat: Adaptive, everywhere
  • Ancestor: Asian Leopard

A crossbreed of a domestic housecat and the Asian Leopard. Unusual for a cat, it loves water and is extremely intelligent. Very active,curious and chatty.
